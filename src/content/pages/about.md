SentryML covers ML observability and MLOps from an engineering-first perspective — model monitoring, drift detection, training/serving skew, deployment reliability, and the operational discipline that keeps ML systems working in production.

## What we cover

- **Model monitoring architecture** — what to instrument, how to structure alerts, and when to retrain
- **Drift detection** — data, concept, and label drift in production ML systems, with tooling recommendations
- **Training/serving skew** — diagnosing the gap between training and serving environments
- **MLOps reliability** — deployment patterns, rollback strategies, and incident response for model failures
- **Observability tooling** — platform comparisons and integration guides for production ML observability stacks

## Who reads this

ML engineers, platform teams, and data scientists responsible for keeping production ML systems reliable. Posts focus on implementation — what to instrument, how to interpret signals, and how to respond when models degrade.
